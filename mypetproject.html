ACE PASTRY CATERING COMPANY

Established by its owner Jane Hannock in 1999, Ace Pastry Catering Company (APCC) is based in Hermitage West Berkshire and is ideally located to serve the Thames Valley, Hampshire, Oxfordshire, Wiltshire and even London (by special request).

APCC can provide bespoke catering services to suit your individual event. They will ensure that each event is meticulously planned down to every last detail all personally supervised by Jane and her very experienced team who will ensure that your event is a total success. APCC has built up an enviable a reputation for high quality food backed by high levels of customer service with a flexible friendly approach that is valued by both private and corporate clients alike.


WHO IS ACE PASTRY

An extremely skilled and creative chef who is passionate about food and customer service, Jane set up her own catering company in 1999 and quickly built a reputation for high quality contemporary cuisine.
